Things to do in Dundee...........

Dundee Ice Area

Dundee Ice Arena is a mutli-purpose arena with an olympic size ice pad, seating capacity for 2,400 spectators, 2 bars & 3 hospitality rooms. Home of Elite League Dundee Stars Ice Hockey Club. Public skating sessions every day..

Call: 01382 889369 or email: info@dundeeicearena.co.uk

http://www.dundeeicearena.co.uk/

Cineworld

Cineworld Cinemas: UK's number 1 multiplex cinema chain. Watch the latest films as well as Bollywood movies, 3D & digital films, IMAX, D-box, theatre, and live music.

Call: 0871 200 2000

www.cineworld.co.uk/

Dundee Contemporary Arts Center

Features international contemporary arts, cinema, shopping, cafe-bar and print studio.

Call: 01382 909900

www.dca.org.uk/
