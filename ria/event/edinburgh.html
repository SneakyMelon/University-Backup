Edinburgh

The May edition of Runner's World magazine carried out extensive research into which of the 10 most popular marathons for UK runners were the fastest. A single comparison (like average time) could skew the results so Runner's World used 200 different comparisons to establish which was the fastest marathon and the Edinburgh Marathon came out as the fastest marathon in the UK.

Overview of Edinburgh Marathon

Flat & fast. This course was voted the fastest marathon in the UK by Runners World in 2008. Great if it's your first marathon or you are looking for a PB! Runners must be a minimum of 18 years old on race day to enter. Please note that the route may be subject to change. The Edinburgh Marathon is a road race over a measured distance of 26.2 miles. It is delivered under permit from Scottish Athletics. The race will be refereed under the rules of Scottish Athletics.
